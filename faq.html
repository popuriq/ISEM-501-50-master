Frequently asked questions

Below you will find a list of the most commonly asked questions we receive from clients during our initial meetings.
Can't find the answer to your question? Please send us message using the contact form. We would be glad to answer your questions,
better yet come down for a consultation and chat with us in person.

Who will photograph our wedding?
When you hire Popuri's Photography to capture your wedding, Venkatesh will be the lead photographer. We do not outsource talent, and only provide services to about 20 weddings per year.

So after the wedding,what do we get?
All of our wedding packages include all photographs delivered in high resolution format. These images will not have any watermarks
or logos on them. The client can print these images as they please for personal use.These are not small, web sized photos photo
but rather full sized images.

Are you available to travel?
We are available to travel throughout the world.
